About Kareem

Hello, my name is Kareem Tarek (aka KareemDEV). I am from Cairo, Egypt and I am a software developer. I specialize in both client-side and server-side development.

I would like to mention that the idea of joining the software development career came to my mind when I was studying Business Administration with a major in Business Information System (BIS) at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AASTMT) university (Heliopolis Campus). The university gave me a good foundation in software development, and encouraged me to keep learning on my own. After graduating, I enrolled in many online and offline courses to gain knowledge in different IT technologies, such as database designing, front-end development, back-end development, native development, frameworks development, and external/third party technologies (e.g. CDNs, libraries & packages, Plugins & Extensions, etc.).

In General, What Would I Be Offering You?
I develop software systems based on custom requirements from clients, which allow users in the system to carry out specific tasks and operations on computers or other devices. These tasks and operations may include input, processing, output, storage, and control activities of the information systems. Authenticated users within the system have different authorizations and permissions or roles to carry out specific tasks and operations in the software systems.
Ex. for some real life applications: Businesses' Dashboards, Reservation/Booking systems, Point of Sale (POS) systems, E-commerce (online shopping) systems, Blog systems, Social Media systems, Inventory systems, Q&A platforms, Event Planning systems (online buyers, services and services' providers), etc.
